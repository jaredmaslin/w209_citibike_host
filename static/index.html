CitiBike Explorer

Created by: Taylor Ma, Jared Maslin, Nihar Patel, and Jasmine Qi

Introduction:

The visualization below is intended for public consumption in order to further educate and encourage data exploration stemming from the Citibike NYC program. The intended audience includes potential users of the Citibike NYC program, as well as those with interest in the program, its usage, or any underlying trends.

Instructions:

To begin exploration, select a single bike station from the map of New York City below. After selecting a bike station, a pop-up window will appear to identify the station selected. Then, make selections from the available filter buttons to the right of the map for further exploration of the data. After define all the options for a station, click the 'submit' button. Below the map displays a histogram with trips by frequency, reflecting the most popular desitinations or origins. Users can also move to a different station at any time by selecting one from the map and updating toggles, as desired.
